Instructions

Welcome to CAPTCHA BONANZA! Follow these instructions to play the game:

  1. On the main game page, you will be presented with a CAPTCHA challenge.
  2. Memorize the characters shown in the CAPTCHA for a few seconds.
  3. Type the memorized characters in the input field provided.
  4. Submit your answer within the given time limit.
  5. Score points for each correct answer.
  6. If you reach a score of 5, you will be declared the winner!
